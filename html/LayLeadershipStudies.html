Lay Leadership Studies Program


SPECIAL PROGRAM



Goals and Objectives

The Lay Leadership Studies Program, rooted in Reformed Biblical Faith, offers a trimester curriculum that provides a summarized yet robust theological education. Designed for young people and church workers, the program integrates theological study with interactive and practical approaches, serving as an introductory ladder toward a bachelor's degree in theology. Completion requires only 18 units, making it accessible and efficient for lay leaders aiming to enhance their ministry capabilities.


1. Foundations in Theology (Reformed Faith): This course provides a thorough study of core theological doctrines based on Scripture, with a focus on:

• God’s sovereignty.
• Covenant theology.
• Doctrines of grace.

Students will also explore Reformed confessions, biblical hermeneutics, and church history to build a strong theological foundation for faith and ministry.

2. The Book of Acts: An in-depth examination of the Acts of the Apostles, this course highlights:

• The growth of the early church.
• Apostolic missionary efforts.
• The work of the Holy Spirit in advancing the gospel.

3. World Mission: This subject covers:

• The biblical mandate for missions.
• Historical developments in Christian missions.
• Effective contemporary mission strategies.

4. Leadership Development: Focused on practical ministry leadership, this course equips students with:

• Principles of biblical leadership.
• Application of leadership skills in diverse ministry contexts.

5. Evangelism and Discipleship: This subject trains students in:

• Principles and practices of gospel proclamation.
• Strategies for mentoring and nurturing believers to grow in their faith.

NOTE:
The program is designed for lay leaders and church workers who desire theological grounding to serve effectively in ministry while balancing their other responsibilities. This curriculum is a step toward fostering a new generation of Biblical leaders equipped to engage and nurture faith communities in a biblically grounded and mission-focused manner.
