Our Vision

    To be a leading Reformed Christian institution in Central Luzon that proclaims the Gospel, ministers to the church, society, and God’s creation

Our Mission

As a Christian institution, Presbyterian Bible Seminary Inc. aims to:

  • • Provide quality and holistic Christian educational services at all levels.
  • • Train men and women for the building-up of the church and transformation of society.
  • • Serve as a catalyst for the expansion of God’s kingdom on earth.
